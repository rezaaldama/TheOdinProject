Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Grease and flour 9x13-inch baking dish.
  2. In a Dutch oven, cook ground beef over medium heat until browned; drain fat. Add onion and garlic; cook until tender.
  3. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, broth, sugar, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper; bring to a boil. Reduce heat; simmer, covered, for about 2 hours.
  4. Place 1 cup meat sauce in the prepared baking dish. Add 1 cup of the ricotta mixture; top with 1 cup of the mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers, ending with a full layer of meat sauce.
  5. Bake in preheated oven for about 25 minutes. Let stand for 15 minutes before serving.
